About Me

Who I Am

I'm Tahir Abbas, an Electrical Engineer from UET Lahore (Class of 2024), currently working as an O&M Engineer at a tracker-based solar plant with Nizam Energy Pvt Ltd in Punjab, Pakistan.

My work spans the full spectrum — from maintaining utility-scale solar systems in the field to building and deploying AI-powered tools using Python and Generative AI. I was recognized as a Top Performer in the PEC × PakAngels GenAI training program.

My Final Year Project was a Humanoid Receptionist Robot — a Raspberry Pi system with face recognition, voice AI, chatbot, and map-based navigation, built from scratch with my team at UET.

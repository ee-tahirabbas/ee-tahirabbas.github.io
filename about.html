About

Two parallel tracks, one engineer.

Most of my work runs on logic and sun. The rest runs on whatever a language model can teach itself overnight.

I graduated from UET Lahore in 2024 with a degree built around power systems, and found two tracks running side by side ever since: keeping a tracker-based solar plant aligned and efficient as an O&M Engineer, and building generative-AI tools that read engineering diagrams, talk back to people, and occasionally drive themselves into walls — looking at you, humanoid robot.


I like systems that close their own loop: trackers that follow the sun, robots that map their own rooms, models that flag a fault before a human spots it. Whether the panel is a switchgear or a chatbot, the question is the same — does it tell you the truth about its own state?
